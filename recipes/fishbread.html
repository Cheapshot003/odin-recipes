Fish in a bread (Fischbrötchen)

Description

This is a recipe on how to make a delicious fish in a bread, also called "Fischbrötchen" in my home. It's really simple. Yummy!

Ingredients

  • Fish (Herring)
  • Bread
  • Some salad
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Get a bread
  2. Cut it in half
  3. Put a fish in there
  4. Put some salad and onions on it
  5. Done!
  6. Optionally: Get a beer
